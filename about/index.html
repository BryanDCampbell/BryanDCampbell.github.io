I'm a Computer Science student at Towson University.

Before I discovered my interest in programming I was pursuing a career in nursing. I started my career as a Licensed Practical Nurse at a pediatric hospital in Baltimore. As I continued through my community college's nursing program to become a Registered Nurse, I realized that my interests were consistently being drawn toward nursing informatics. The further I delved into research on the subject, the more I felt that I was on the wrong side of the field.

After consulting with a good friend whose judgement I trust, I came to the decision to pursue programming in earnest. Every step from that point forward has lead to here and now with you viewing my portfolio.

When I'm not studying or hard at work, I play videogames, explore walking trails in nearby parks, discover new places to eat in Baltimore, and obsess a little too much over fantasy football.
