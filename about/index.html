I'm a Computer Science student at Towson University.

Before I discovered my interest in programming I was pursuing a career in nursing. Enrolled in the nursing program at my local community college, I earned my nursing license and started my career as an LPN. As I was continuing the program to become an RN I realized that my interests were consistently drawn toward nursing informatics. The further I delved into research on the subject, the more I felt that I was on the wrong side of the field.

After consulting with a good friend who's judgement I trust, I came to the decision to pursue programming in earnest. Every step from that point forward has lead to here and now with you viewing my portfolio.
