I'm a Computer Science student at Towson University.

Before I discovered my interest in programming I was pursuing a career in nursing. I've earned my nursing license and started my career as an LPN at a pediatric hospital in Baltimore. As I continued through a community college nursing program to become an RN, I realized that my interests were consistently being drawn toward nursing informatics. The further I delved into research on the subject, the more I felt that I was on the wrong side of the field.

After consulting with a good friend who's judgement I trust, I came to the decision to pursue programming in earnest. Every step from that point forward has lead to here and now with you viewing my portfolio.
